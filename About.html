ABOUT US

Welcome to SadGhost Studio, an indie game company where creativity and fun come first.
We're a small team of three individuals who work on our games during our spare time while maintaining full-time jobs.
Despite the challenges that come with balancing work and our passion for game development,
we're dedicated to creating enjoyable experiences for players of all ages.

Our team at SadGhost Studio is united by a shared passion for creativity and an unwavering commitment tomaking games
that are not only fun to play but also visually stunning. We believe that everyone deserves to have a little fun in their lives,
and we strive to make games that bring joy to people from all walks of life.

We hope you'll take the time to check out our games and see the passion and hard work that we put into each and
every one of them. Thank you for your support, and we can't wait to share our next creation with you!
