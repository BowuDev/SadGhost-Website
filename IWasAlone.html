I WAS ALONE

Survive. Adapt. Uncover the truth.
"I Was Alone" is a relentless post-apocalyptic survival game that plunges you into a world torn apart by an enigmatic catastrophe.
In a desolate landscape filled with undead horrors, you'll need every ounce of your ingenuity, resourcefulness, and combat skills to stay alive.

Dynamic Open World

Explore a procedurally generated open world, from decaying urban ruins to haunting wilderness, each with its own challenges and secrets.

Deep Progression

Earn XP by looting, hunting, and completing quests. Level up to unlock new crafting recipes, skills, and abilities, enhancing your chances of survival.

The haunting soundtrack and atmospheric sound effects immerse you in the desolation of the world, while dynamic music keeps you on your toes.
